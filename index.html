Browser Tycoon

Turn everyday browsing into progression. Assign websites to slots, earn money from the domains you use, buy upgrades, build a stronger portfolio, and clear cache for permanent power.

Load the extension See the game loop

Browse. Upgrade. Prestige.

Browser Tycoon is built around the sites you already visit. Each domain becomes a little business with active income, background rewards, vault payouts, and its own upgrade tree.

Slot favorite domains

Add sites like video, docs, mail, or work tools into limited slots and choose which domains deserve attention.

Earn while browsing

Focused tabs, background tabs, navigation, daily visits, and vault returns all feed your run economy.

Clear Cache to grow

Prestige resets convert lifetime progress into Cache Points for stronger future runs and permanent slot power.
